About EDUSIS e-Library

EDUSIS is a project designed to give secondary school students in Tanzania (and beyond) equitable access to high-quality learning materials. It sits under the Secondary Education Students Information System and is powered by NAT-LABs — focused on accessibility, curriculum-alignment and sustainability.
